Reheating Your Burnt Ends

Make sure your Kansas City burnt ends taste just as incredible at home. Here's exactly how to reheat them like a pitmaster:

🔥 Oven method: 350°F for 15-20 minutes, covered with foil
💧 Add 2 tbsp of water to keep them moist
🍯 Brush with molasses sauce in the last 5 minutes
⏰ Let rest 5 minutes before serving
